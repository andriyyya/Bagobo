About the Bagobo

The Bagobo are indigenous people mainly living in Davao and other parts of Mindanao, Philippines.

Culture

Music, dance, rituals, and weaving are essential parts of Bagobo heritage.

Clothing

The Bagobo are known for woven abaca garments, beadwork, embroidery, and brass ornaments.

Beliefs

The Bagobo traditionally believe in nature spirits, gods, and ancestral beings.

Location

The Bagobo traditionally live around Mount Apo and in parts of Davao Region, Mindanao.
